The Ford Ranger is a versatile midsize pickup truck with a storied history dating back to 1982. Originally introduced as a compact truck, it evolved over the decades to become a robust and capable vehicle. Known for its durability and performance, the Ranger offers a balance of rugged utility and modern technology, making it a popular choice for both work and adventure. The current generation features advanced safety systems, comfortable interiors, and powerful engine options that deliver impressive towing capacity. Whether you're hauling equipment to a job site or embarking on an off-road adventure, the Ranger delivers the capability and reliability that Ford truck owners have come to expect over the decades.
